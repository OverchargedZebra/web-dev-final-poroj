Character introduction

Name: Alisa Bosconovitch

Fighting style: Robotics/All-round

Difficulty: Moderate

Strengths:

• Excellent movement
• Safe moves
• Good mix-up potential
• Strong wall game
• Unique chainsaw attacks

Key moves:

• d/f+1: A safe mid kick that can be used to start combos or poke the opponent.
• f+4: A long-range mid kick that can be used to punish whiffed attacks.
• 1, 2, 1+2: A wall combo that deals a lot of damage and activates Alisa's destructive form.
• d/b+2, 1: A low kick that can be used to start combos or mix up the opponent.
• d/f+1, 4: A mid-high extension that can be used to catch opponents ducking or stepping.
• d/1+2: Activates Alisa's destructive form, which gives her access to new moves and combos.

Weaknesses:

• Low damage output
• Weak throw game
• Some moves are punishable at high levels of play

Optimization:

• Alisa's main strength is her movement. Use her backdashes and sidesteps to evade attacks and get into position to punish your opponent.
• Alisa has a lot of safe moves. Use these moves to pressure your opponent and force them to make a mistake.
• Alisa's mix-up potential is excellent. Use her lows, mids, and throws to keep your opponent guessing.
• Alisa's wall game is very strong. Once you get your opponent against the wall, use her chainsaw attacks to deal a lot of damage.

Here are some additional tips for playing Alisa:

• Use d/f+1 to start combos or poke the opponent.
• Use f+4 to punish whiffed attacks and keep your opponent at a distance.
• Use 1, 2, 1+2 to deal a lot of damage and activate your destructive form.
• Use d/b+2, 1 to start combos or mix up the opponent with a low kick.
• Use d/f+1, 4 to catch opponents ducking or stepping.
• Use d/1+2 to activate your destructive form and gain access to new moves and combos.
• Be aware of Alisa's low damage output and weak throw game.
• Use Alisa's movement, safe moves, mix-up potential, and strong wall game to your advantage.
• With practice, you can become a master of Alisa Bosconovitch and win matches against even the most skilled opponents
