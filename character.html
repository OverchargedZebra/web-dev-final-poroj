How to choose a character

How to choose a character in Tekken 7 Tekken 7 is a fighting game with a huge roster of characters, each with their own unique playstyle. Choosing the right character for you can be daunting, but there are a few things you can consider to narrow down your options.

Reason for playing What is your reason for playing Tekken 7? Are you looking to win at all costs, or are you more interested in having fun and expressing yourself? If you're looking to win, you'll want to choose a character that is strong and well-rounded. If you're more interested in having fun, you can choose a character that you like the look of or that has a playstyle that appeals to you.

Character ease of use Some characters in Tekken 7 are easier to learn and use than others. If you're new to the game, you might want to choose a character that is considered to be beginner-friendly. Some good beginner characters include:

-- Asuka Kazama

Character styles Tekken 7 characters can be divided into different playstyles. Some characters are good at close-range combat, while others are better at fighting from a distance. Some characters are counterattackers, while others are more aggressive. Here is a brief overview of the different character:

All-rounders: These characters are good at all aspects of combat and have no major weaknesses. Some examples of all-rounders include Jin Kazama, Paul Phoenix, and Kazuya Mishima.

Close-range fighters: These characters excel at close-range combat and have strong punches and kicks. Some examples of close-range fighters include King, Lei Wulong, and Steve Fox.

Long-range fighters: These characters are better at fighting from a distance and have long-reaching moves. Some examples of long-range fighters include Nina Williams, Bryan Fury, and Miguel Caballero Rojo.

Counterattackers: These characters are good at waiting for their opponent to attack and then counterattacking with powerful moves. Some examples of counterattackers are Yoshimitsu, Feng Wei, and Claudio Serafino.

Grapplers: These characters specialize in grappling and throwing their opponents. Some examples of grapplers are King, Gigas, and Kuma.
